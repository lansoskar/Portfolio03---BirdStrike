The Financial Impact Of Bird Strikes

There has been a large amount of bird strikes reported since 1990. Above we see the reported amount for each month, which shows an especially large amount of reports in the most popular travelling months. Bird strikes are a common case and almost unavoidable in the current aviation climate - which may be a cause for concern if you look below.

Above we see the ten most expensive reported bird strikes, hitting as much as more than 45 million USD. When a bird strike has done enough damage to warrant repairment; the average inflation adjusted repairment price since 1990 is at 171.197 USD. These are large numbers for a relatively common occurrence that could be avoided in the future.
